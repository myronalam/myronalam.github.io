Join the Band

The DRCB rehearses weekly on Thursdays from 7-9 pm, typically in the Martinez Adult Education multipurpose room.

If you are interested in joining the band or would like more information, please call Cora to verify the rehearsal location and time.

Donations

The DRBC is an all volunteer organization that depends on the support of the community. We purchase our own music and equipment, and we welcome any donations to help us.

We primarily rely on contributions to our tip jar at performances. We also offer opportunties for individuals and businesses to support us in other ways. You can have your name or logo featured in our concert programs, or you can book us for a special event in the East Bay.

We appreciate any support we receive. If you are interested in supporting us or learning more about how you can help, please call Cora.
